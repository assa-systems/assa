What ASSA Does

Everything your campus shuttle needs

Purpose-built for the AFIT community with real-time tracking, offline support, and smart ride management.

🛺
Instant Ride Requests

Request a campus shuttle from any pickup point — AFIT Gate, BK Gate, Old Girls Hostel, Boys Hostel — with one tap.

📶
Works Offline

Firestore offline caching keeps the app functional even when your connection drops. Syncs automatically when you're back online.

🔔
Push Notifications

Get notified when your driver accepts or arrives via Firebase Cloud Messaging push notifications.

🛰️
ESP32 / LoRa IoT

Integrates with on-campus ESP32 hardware via 6-byte binary LoRa packets for real-time shuttle tracking at the hardware level.

🧩
Campus Puzzle & Lost & Found

Built-in community features — a daily puzzle game and a lost-and-found board to keep the AFIT community connected.

🔐
Secure Auth

Email/password and Google Sign-In with verified accounts. Phone OTP authentication is built and ready for when billing is enabled.

💬
Support Chat

Submit complaints with live status tracking — Open, In Progress, Resolved. Get updates directly in the app.

How It Works

From gate to destination in three steps

Designed for simplicity. Whether you're a student or driver, ASSA fits your workflow.

1

Sign Up or Sign In

Create an account with your email or Google account. Choose your role — Rider or Driver — and you're in.

2

Request Your Ride

Select your pickup location and drop-off. The nearest available driver gets notified instantly.

3

Track & Ride

Follow your driver's status in real time. Get push notifications at every step of the journey.

📱 iPhone Users — How to Install ASSA

No App Store needed. Works like a native app on your iPhone.

1

Open Safari on your iPhone

Must be Safari — Chrome and Firefox do not support installation

2

Go to this link in Safari

assa-systems.github.io/assa/app/

3

Tap the Share button

The box with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of Safari

4

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the Share menu to find this option

5

Tap "Add" — done!

ASSA icon appears on your home screen. Open it like any app.
